Privacy

Last updated: June 2026

D'Vaaw is based in Kota Samarahan, Sarawak, Malaysia. We respect your privacy and only collect what we need to fulfil your order.

What we collect

  • Details you send us on WhatsApp (name, phone, delivery address) to process and ship your order.
  • Order records, kept securely for fulfilment and record-keeping.
  • Your shopping cart and preferences, remembered on your device for your convenience.

What we never do

We don't sell your data, run advertising trackers, or share your information with third parties beyond what's needed to deliver your parcel.

Your account

If you create a D'Vaaw account, your password is protected by industry-standard encryption and is never visible to us — not even our own team can read it. Your profile details (name, age, preferences) are used only to personalise your experience and are never sold or shared.

One good habit we encourage: use a password that's different from your email account's password. If you ever want your account and data deleted, just message us on WhatsApp and we'll remove it.

Cookies & Preferences

We don't use tracking or advertising cookies. We only remember your cart and recently viewed items so your experience stays smooth between visits. You can clear this anytime through your browser settings.

Terms of Sale

  • All orders are confirmed personally via WhatsApp before any payment is made.
  • Prices are in Malaysian Ringgit (RM) and include applicable taxes.
  • We reserve the right to decline or cancel an order if a product is unavailable.
  • Fragrance descriptions are guides — scent is personal and may vary by individual.

Shipping & Returns

We ship within Malaysia only. Sarawak: RM10 flat (1kg). Semenanjung: RM15/kg. Sabah & Labuan: quote on request.

As fragrances are personal-care items, we accept returns only for damaged or incorrect items. Message us on WhatsApp within 3 days of delivery with a photo and we'll make it right.
