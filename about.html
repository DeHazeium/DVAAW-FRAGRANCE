Our Origin

Why Gula Apong?

Gula Apong — Nypa palm sugar — is one of Sarawak's most treasured natural gifts. Gentle, long-lasting, deeply rooted in the land. The very qualities we wanted in every bottle.

Choosing it wasn't just picking an ingredient. It was a declaration: luxury doesn't have to come from elsewhere. It can come from here. From Borneo.

Our Mission

Uplift. Empower. Inspire.

D'Vaaw exists to create meaningful livelihoods for single mothers and students in Sarawak. Every bottle sold contributes to this mission.

When someone wears D'Vaaw, they wear proof that community, resilience, and beauty can share the same breath.
